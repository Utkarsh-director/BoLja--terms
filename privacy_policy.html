Section 01

Introduction

BoLja! ("we", "our", or "the app") is a social Q&A platform that allows users to post questions, share concerns, and engage with their community.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using BoLja!, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
Section 02

Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Full Name — entered during profile setup
  • Location — city/area you choose to display
  • Profile Information — name and location shown on your posts

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Google Account data — email address and Google profile (via Google Sign-In)
  • Unique Account ID — assigned automatically at login to link your activity
  • Push notification token — used to send notifications to your device
  • Posts, comments, and replies — all content you create in the app
  • Likes — posts you have liked

c) Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your phone number
  • We do not collect your location in real-time (GPS)
  • We do not collect your contacts or call logs
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect device hardware identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)

Section 04

Push Notifications

  • BoLja! sends push notifications when someone likes, comments on, or replies to your post
  • Notifications require your explicit permission — you can allow or deny them at any time
  • Your push notification token is stored securely in our database and is only used to deliver notifications to your device

How to Disable Notifications

  • App Settings → Notifications → Disable
  • Phone Settings → Apps → BoLja! → Notifications → Off
Section 05

Cache & Offline Storage

To provide a smooth experience — even when your internet connection is slow or unavailable — BoLja! saves certain data directly on your device using your browser's or app's local storage. This means the feed will not be blank when you open the app offline.

What We Store on Your Device

Data Stored Locally Purpose Shared with Server?
Cached Posts Feed Show previously loaded posts when offline No
Your Posts Cache Show your own posts instantly without re-loading No
Session State (name, account ID, profile photo URL) Keep you logged in between app opens without requiring re-authentication No — stored locally only
T&C / Privacy Policy Acceptance Flag Remember that you have already agreed so we do not prompt you again No
Comment Seen Count Show unread comment badge on posts you have not yet opened No

Important Points

  • All locally cached data stays on your device only — it is never uploaded to our servers separately
  • Cached data contains only publicly available post content and minimal session info needed to keep you logged in
  • No passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal data are ever stored in local cache
  • Cache is automatically refreshed with fresh data every time you reconnect to the internet
  • When you log out or delete your account, all locally cached data is cleared from your device immediately

How to Clear Cache Manually

  • App Settings → Clear Cache
  • Phone Settings → Apps → BoLja! → Storage → Clear Cache
Why we do this: So your feed loads instantly and the screen is never empty — even with a slow or no internet connection. No extra data is collected; this is purely for your offline experience.
Section 06

Data Storage & Security

  • All server data is stored on secure cloud servers with industry-standard encryption
  • We use strict access controls to ensure users can only access their own private data
  • Data in transit between your device and our servers is protected using HTTPS encryption
  • Data may be stored on servers located outside your country, subject to Google's data handling standards
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.
Section 07

Google Sign-In

  • BoLja! uses Google OAuth for authentication — no passwords are stored by us
  • When you sign in with Google, we receive your: name, email address, and Google profile photo URL
  • We do not access your Google contacts, Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service beyond basic profile information
Google's own Privacy Policy applies to your Google account data: policies.google.com/privacy
Section 08

User-Generated Content

  • Everything you post (questions, comments, replies) is publicly visible to all users of the app
  • Do not share personal information (phone numbers, addresses, financial information) in your posts
  • We reserve the right to remove any content that violates our Terms & Conditions
Reminder: Public posts are also locally cached on other users' devices for offline viewing. Avoid sharing any information in posts that you would not want temporarily stored on another person's device.
Section 09

Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, your profile and posts will be permanently removed from our servers
  • Notification tokens are deleted when you log out or delete your account
  • All locally cached data on your device is cleared when you log out or delete your account

Children's Privacy
