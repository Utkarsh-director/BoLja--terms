Section 11

Offline Functionality

BoLja! supports offline posting. When your device is not connected to the internet, posts you submit are temporarily saved on your device.

  • Offline posts are automatically uploaded when your device reconnects to the internet
  • You may also manually trigger the upload via the "Upload Now" button in the pending posts banner
  • Offline posts are stored only on your local device until uploaded — they are not accessible to other users or BoLja! servers until sync occurs
  • If you clear your device's app data before syncing, any unsent offline posts will be permanently lost
  • Previously loaded posts are also cached locally so your feed is not empty when offline
Note: The same content policies apply to offline posts. Posts queued offline that violate these Terms will be subject to the same moderation actions upon upload as any other post.
